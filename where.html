# Where in the world? --- Tundra is a quite big biome though you might wonder where can you find it? Tundra is usually found in the north, in the regions right below the ice caps of the Arctic, extending in to the North America, into Europe and Siriya which is in Asia. So in short terms the tundra biome can be found under the ice caps in the colder parts of the earth at around 60° to 75° of latitude. Talking about the countries tundra can be found in America, coasts of Greenland, Russia.
